Company Profile

We, Omega Bath Solutions Pvt. Ltd. and ISO 9001:2008 Certified company, Our Brand "APPOLLO" was established in 2004 and is based on the spirit of artisans. After more than 17 years of development, Appollo people rely on “continuous improvement and innovation”.  Appollo Brand is a large-scale modern enterprise specializing in R&D, production and sales of high-end leisure sanitary ware Wellness Products. The entrepreneurial spirit continues to expand the scale of the company and create one after another. Appollo is the distinguished Manufacturer, Supplier, Retailer, Service Provider and Trader of Modular Bath Solutions in New Delhi. We are offering a broad spectrum of Steam Shower Room, Steam Bath Generator, Sauna Bath, Whirlpool Massage Bathtub, SPA Hot Tubs, Water Chillers, Pressure Pump, Water Sofetner and Glass Shower Enclosure in New Delhi.
